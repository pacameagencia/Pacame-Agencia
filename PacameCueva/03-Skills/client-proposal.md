# Proposal Structure (3-Act Narrative)

## Act 1 — Mirror (Show you understand their world)
- Reflect the client's situation in their language
- Name the specific pain points they described
- Quantify the cost of inaction (lost revenue, wasted time)

## Act 2 — Solution (Map each capability to a pain)
- Present services as a journey, not a list
- Each service solves a specific problem from Act 1
- Include timeline with milestones
- Be specific: "We'll build X using Y, delivering Z by date"

## Act 3 — Future State (Paint the outcome)
- Concrete metrics: traffic, conversions, revenue
- Timeline to results (be honest, not optimistic)
- Social proof: similar results for comparable businesses

# Lead Qualification (ICP Scoring 1-5)
Score the lead on these criteria:
- Budget alignment (can they afford PACAME services?)
- Timeline urgency (need it now vs. someday)
- Decision maker (talking to the person who signs?)
- Digital maturity (do they understand what they need?)
- Growth potential (will they need more services later?)

Score 4+ = hot lead → notify Pablo immediately
Score 2-3 = nurture → add to email sequence
Score 1 = not a fit → politely decline
